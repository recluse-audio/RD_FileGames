JOURNAL ENTRY
2011-08-07

Still feel weird writing these journal entries, but pastor Eric said we all have to do it so we can tell our story to the church back home. So, here goes.

I woke up today underneath my mosquito net.
I was confused at first, like, "this isn't my bedroom"
lol!
I keep forgetting I'm on a completely different continent!

But who can blame me, not many young Christian men 
born in the standard Tri-state trivium of the USA are 
spending their summer before their senior year 
helping poor, impoverished, unintelligent, sub-human,
adorable Africans make mud huts!

Yesterday we built an entire hut out of mud, only to
realize as we were thatching the roof with native grasses that the "mud" we used was actually elephant shit. The smell of feces and presence of flies is quite pervasive in the 3rd world, and I as a Christian do not complain.

There was a strange man who came today, from West Africa. I would not have recognized the difference if
not for the strange behavior of the locals. As he stepped out of his Toyota Hilux, the whole village disappeared into every available nook and cranny in the  dilapidated hovels.

The man had military style sash on and a beret. Two large men resembling rhinocerous carried ak-47's as the man who appeared a general sauntered towards the village center.

"Where ah my beautiful ladies, and my hardworking men, and my sweet innocent children" he beckoned invitingly. Yet the village people stayed sequestred light cockroaches evading a light. 

"Oh, they won't come huh, well let's go looking for them" - and at this sardonic phrase spoken half playfully, the squad of leather skinned abominations began to tear through the pitiful pueblos made of animal shit.

"And what do we have here, a WHITE one" - he said finally noticing me.

"Hi, uhhhh, I'm Cam". I sputtered out.

"Caaammmm! you must be American Cam? Mickey 
Mouse and Coca Cola right?"

"ha, yeah, mickey mouse and the simpsons and coke"

"wait, you like the simpsons?" - I nodded - "who is your favorite character?"

while he asked me this I could hear the discovery and capture of the first of many victims. It was a mother and her son, a boy maybe 11 years old. He held a shoddy spear with no spearhead affixed yet. His brave and futile attempt to defend his mother, the woman whom he loved the most in the world. He had been fighting imaginary beasts with it only days before, still a child in the world of his mind. Now the boy would face the cruel realities of his farcical fantasy.

The first of the Rhino men pushed his useless spear aside and drove a short hunting knife into the child's stomach. The boy fell to the ground doubled over choking on his own blood and bleeding out slowly. Spending his last glances on watching his mother be raped to death by the massive erect penises of the warlord's servants.

"Come, somewhere with less distractions my friend."
He gestured towards a two tone cream and powder blue Rolls Royce with Ghanian diplomat flags above either headlights. Stenciled on with spray paint on either side door were the words GHANIAN HUMAN CAPITAL EXCHANGE.

I was very concerned for the mud huts I had built the previous day, but he pulled me along into the luxury vehicle. The interior was a shocking relief against the earthy dwellings of the local Tanzanians.

This man told me everything about the history of Ghana and it's favorite industry, slaving. He told me about selling Africans of all kinds to "Brazilians" from Portugal. I don't remember much about this man in specific. Except that his name was Appouh. I do remember however, the power, the force, the hatred, and the tenderness in his eyes. He took my virginity in that Rolls Royce today. I don't know if I'll ever see him again, but I'll be chanting the name Appouh in my mind until I find him, and once again reach the ultimate climax.
